FAQs

Are there any prerequisites or required skills?

There are no pre-requisites or prior skills required to enrol in this event. This event will include marvellous technical workshops which will help increase your skills in various niches, at the end of which there will be a Hackathon.

Do I need to be a part of a team?

Team Size: 1-3 Members – You can either enter alone or with a team of maximum three members.

What is the registration fee for the event?

The registration fee differs according to the number of members in your team:

  • 1 Member – Rs. 60
  • 2 Members – Rs. 110
  • 3 Members – Rs. 150
What perks and goodies will I get if I register for the event?

Once you register for this event, you will gain access to all of the workshops and will be enrolled as a participant for the Hackathon. There is something for everyone! Even if you don’t win the Hackathon, you have the opportunity of gaining 7 Certificates, and deliverables worth Rs.30,000!

Check the Prizes section for more Details.

What platform will the event be conducted on?

The event will be conducted on Discord and Zoom. All communication and workshop links will be provided to the participants on the Discord Platform.

Can students from different colleges form a team?

Yes! This event is open to all college students, and you may form a team with members from different colleges.
